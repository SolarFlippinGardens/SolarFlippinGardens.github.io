Solar Flippin' Gardens

Solar Flippin' Gardens ™ (SFG) is a project to investigate the use of solar panels in gardens. They should not be walked on, or even remotly driven upon, they are not traffic bearing. Our panels contain sexy color chaging LED lights to create a 70s style disco effect in your garden without needing a single glitter ball. They can provide an almost straight line border from LEDs for your gardens to simulate a nicely finished edging. The shape of these panels allows for random plants to be placed at the back of the panel run to hide the gaps. Many people are shocked to learn these panels are made of crappy thin acrylic, not that nice high quality acrylic they sell on eBay. SFG panels have been tested and shown to support the weight of a cup of tea so the odd frog on the panel should be no problem. You can read more about the panel on our Specifics page. We are in the very early stage of development, absolutly nothing but a bit of fun is expected to come of this project. Usage:

  • These solar panels can be used to replace garden borders, normally reserved for plants.
  • Panels do not take load and should not be used for paving or a roadway.
  • They can be placed in dark areas and provide fun lighting.

Low Maintenance:

  • No need to clear the snow off the panels, they self-heat to even maybe melt the snow.
  • Water proof! Tested to a depth of 0.000mm these panels can take a good splashing of water (provided it hits the top surface only and not the sides).
  • No need to cut the grass with these panels, they are ever-green (during night hours with green LEDs selected, green in no way refers to environmental credentials.)
  • Weed free! Maybe a few bugs in the electronics, nothing will grow on these panels.
  • During the summer your solar garden may even generate enough elecity to run a small micro-controller.
