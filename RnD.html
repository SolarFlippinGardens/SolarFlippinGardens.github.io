Research:

We've researched lots, like the very well known fact that tilted solar panels work best, and panels covered in snow generate no electricity, so, we're going ahead with laying them them flat and covering them in acrylic to cut down the light that can get to the panel. We might set-up some test sites to collect data for SFG0001 panels, one in Cambridge UK, one in Essex. Don't expect to see too much data from these sites from our actual panels...

I'd like to thank the U.S. Department of Transportation and the 2-year $750,000 budget for that extensive research.
